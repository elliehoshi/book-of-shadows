Book of Shadows is an online publication that examines the creative process by unpacking ritual and symbol. The sacred is not as obscure as it seems, all the objects we choose to keep are charged with meaning and influence that impact our thoughts and work. Book of Shadows aims to find magic and meaning in our daily surroundings to be shared as inspiration and revelation.
